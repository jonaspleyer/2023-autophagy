Cargo or R11 particle depending on the Species of the interaction field.

Fields§

§mechanics: Langevin3D

The [Langevin3D] motion was chosen to model mechanics of the particle movement

§interaction: TypedInteraction

The TypedInteraction assigns the Species to the particle and handles calculation of interactions.
